# Spicy Cubes P&L Exclusions

This document explains the expenses excluded from the final P&L. These are either owner-specific add-backs or non-recurring costs that will not continue under new ownership.

## Overview

Total excluded: **$167,043**

These exclusions transform `2_transformed_pnl.csv` into `3_final_pnl.csv`.

**Note:** Exclusions were only applied to the trailing twelve months (TTM). Earlier periods were not reviewed for exclusions.

---

## Non-Recurring Expenses

### One-Time Project — $44,531

Branding and design work including logo development, packaging design, and brand identity. These foundational assets are already created and won't need to be redone.

### Legal — $27,000

One-time legal expenses for tax law and FTC compliance matters that have been resolved.

### One-Time COGS — $22,856

Rush fees paid to our manufacturer to expedite production during a presale period when we were at risk of missing delivery commitments. This was a one-time operational issue that has been resolved with improved inventory planning.

### Refunds — ($2,225)

Two Upwork contractor refunds. These were credits received, shown as negative expenses. Excluded because they offset one-time contractor issues that have been resolved.

All discretionary spending expenses (14 transactions) that are not required for business operations. This includes owner purchases and non-essential spending run through the business.
