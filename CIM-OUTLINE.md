# CIM (Confidential Information Memorandum) Outline

## Financial Deep Dive
- [ ] Full monthly P&L with line-item detail (available at /pnl)
- [ ] Balance sheet (cash, inventory, liabilities)
- [ ] Cash flow statement
- [ ] Working capital analysis (inventory turns, payment terms)
- [ ] Customer acquisition cost (CAC) and lifetime value (LTV) breakdown
- [ ] Unit economics per SKU

## Business Operations
- [ ] Detailed product catalog with margins per SKU
- [ ] Supplier details and terms
- [ ] Fulfillment/3PL setup and costs
- [ ] Inventory management process
- [ ] Return/refund rates

## Content & Creator Details
- [ ] Creator roster with performance metrics
- [ ] Contract terms and commission structures
- [ ] Content production workflow

- [ ] Proprietary software demo/screenshots

## Customer Data
- [ ] Customer demographics
- [ ] Repeat purchase rate
- [ ] Email/SMS list size and engagement
- [ ] Review ratings and sentiment

## Risk Factors
- [ ] Platform dependency
- [ ] Key person risk
- [ ] Supplier concentration
- [ ] Regulatory (FDA/FTC for supplements)

## Deal Terms
- [ ] Asking price / multiple
- [ ] Deal structure (asset vs stock sale)
